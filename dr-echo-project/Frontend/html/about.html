About Dr. Echo

A simple AI-assisted platform built to make radiology reporting clearer, faster, and more consistent.

What We Do

Dr. Echo helps radiologists generate structured medical reports using ready-made templates and AI-assisted terminology suggestions. Our goal is to simplify the reporting workflow and improve clarity for clinicians.

Our Mission

To support better communication in healthcare by making radiology reports more organized, accurate, and easy to understand.

Who We Are

We are a team of IT students from Taylor's University, guided by clinical advisors and academic supervisors, developing Dr. Echo as our Final Year Project.

Built with Passion & Purpose

Dr. Echo represents the intersection of healthcare innovation and student ambition. Our multidisciplinary team combines technical expertise with clinical insights to create a tool that truly serves the medical community.

Advanced structured reporting solutions for radiologists and healthcare facilities.
